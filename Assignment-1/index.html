Institute of Engineering & Technology, DAVV

About Us

The Institute of Engineering & Technology (IET), DAVV, Indore, is a premier institute providing engineering education with a focus on academic excellence and research. Established in 1997, IET DAVV offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in different disciplines of engineering.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution that contributes to the advancement of knowledge, skills, and technologies in the field of engineering and technology.

Our Mission

  • To provide world-class education and research opportunities in the field of engineering.
  • To foster critical thinking and innovation in all our students.
  • To enhance the employability and leadership potential of students.

Departments

Computer Engineering

The Computer Engineering department emphasizes areas such as Software Development, Data Structures, and Algorithms, along with emerging technologies like AI and Machine Learning.

Key Research Areas:

  • AI & Machine Learning
  • Big Data & Data Science
  • Cybersecurity

Information Technology

This department focuses on Information Systems, Cloud Computing, and Internet of Things (IoT), providing hands-on experience in the latest IT technologies.

Key Research Areas:

  • Cloud Computing
  • IoT & Embedded Systems
  • Blockchain Technology

Electronics & Communication

Offering programs related to Electronics, Telecommunication, and Signal Processing, the department focuses on practical training and research in communication technologies.

Key Research Areas:

  • Signal Processing
  • Telecommunication
  • Embedded Systems

Mechanical Engineering

Focusing on innovations in mechanical systems, automation, and thermal engineering, this department prepares students for careers in industries like manufacturing and robotics.

Key Research Areas:

  • Robotics
  • Automation
  • Thermal Engineering

Civil Engineering

This department specializes in Infrastructure development, Environmental Engineering, and Structural Design, with an emphasis on sustainability and smart cities.

Key Research Areas:

  • Structural Engineering
  • Geotechnical Engineering
  • Environmental Engineering

Admissions

Admission to various undergraduate and postgraduate courses is based on merit, entrance exams, and university guidelines. The process ensures transparency and provides ample opportunities for deserving candidates.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • For Undergraduate Programs: A minimum of 50% marks in 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as subjects.
  • For Postgraduate Programs: A B.Tech/BE degree with a minimum of 60% marks in the relevant discipline.

Admission Procedure:

  • Step 1: Fill the online application form.
  • Step 2: Appear for the entrance exam if required.
  • Step 3: Document verification and seat allotment.
  • Step 4: Confirmation and fee payment.
